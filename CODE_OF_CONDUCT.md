# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation
in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and respectful. Assume good faith.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what's best for the community and for users of mello.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments.
- Public or private harassment of any participant.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **admin@pasmello.com**. Reports are
confidential and will be reviewed by the project maintainers within 72
hours. Consequences may include a warning, temporary ban, or permanent
ban from the community — maintainers will choose proportionally.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — the repo, issue
tracker, PR reviews, Discord/chat if any — and also to public spaces when
an individual is representing the project.
